# AI PROMPTS FOR GRANT GENERATION

Use these prompts with me to generate grant sections.

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Write a 250-word EXECUTIVE SUMMARY for a grant application.

Organization: [NAME]
Mission: [MISSION]
Project: [PROJECT NAME]
Amount Requested: $[AMOUNT]
What it does: [DESCRIPTION]
Expected outcomes: [OUTCOMES]

Make it compelling and concise. Include the ask amount.

---

## NEED STATEMENT

Write a compelling NEED STATEMENT.

Organization: [NAME]
Problem: [PROBLEM]
Who is affected: [POPULATION]
Current data/statistics: [DATA]
Why it matters now: [WHY NOW]

Use specific data. Make it urgent.

---

## PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Write the PROJECT DESCRIPTION section.

Activities: [WHAT WILL HAPPEN]
Timeline: [WHEN]
Location: [WHERE]
Staff: [WHO]
Partners: [PARTNERS]

Use active verbs. Be specific.

---

## METHODOLOGY

Write the METHODOLOGY section.

Approach: [HOW]
Activities: [LIST]
Sequence: [ORDER]
Staff qualifications: [RESUMES]
Why your org is qualified: [EXPERIENCE]

---

## EVALUATION PLAN

Write an EVALUATION PLAN.

Outcomes: [EXPECTED CHANGES]
Indicators: [HOW WE MEASURE]
Data collection: [METHODS]
Baseline: [CURRENT DATA]
Targets: [GOALS]

Include both numbers (quantitative) and stories (qualitative).

---

## SUSTAINABILITY

Write how this project continues after grant ends.

Current funding: [NOW]
After grant: [FUTURE]
Other sources: [OTHER FUNDING]
Long-term impact: [IMPACT]

---

## BUDGET NARRATIVE

Write justification for each budget line.

Personnel: [WHO + ROLE + RATE + HOURS]
Fringe: [RATE APPLIED]
Consultants: [WHY NEEDED + COST]
Equipment: [JUSTIFICATION]
Supplies: [ITEMIZE]
Travel: [PURPOSE]
Indirect: [RATE + BASE]

---

## FULL GRANT DRAFT

To generate a complete grant, provide:

1. Organization name and mission
2. Grant program and funder
3. Amount requested
4. Project description
5. Need/problem statement
6. Target population
7. Activities/timeline
8. Budget breakdown
9. Evaluation approach
10. Any funder priorities from their RFP

I will then generate all narrative sections.

---

## COMMON GRANT TYPES

### CDBG (Community Development Block Grant)
Focus: Low/moderate income beneficiaries, community development, public facilities

### ESG (Emergency Solutions Grant)
Focus: Homelessness prevention, rapid re-housing, street outreach

### HOME Investment Partnerships
Focus: Affordable housing, homeowner assistance, rental development

### Public Housing Capital Fund
Focus: Physical improvements, energy efficiency, modernization

### HUD Section 8/HCV
Focus: Housing choice, administrative efficiency, participant services

### 501(c)(3) Corporate Grants
Focus: Mission alignment, measurable outcomes, organizational capacity
