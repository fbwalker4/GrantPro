How Grant Pro Works

From sign-up to submission in 8 steps. See exactly how the platform guides you through every stage of a winning grant application.

1

Create Your Profile Free

Your organization profile is the foundation for everything Grant Pro does. The more complete it is, the better your AI-generated content and the more accurate your eligibility matching.

  • Sign up with your email — no credit card required
  • Complete your organization profile: name, EIN, UEI, address, and mission statement
  • Fill out the Grant Readiness Profile: applicant type, SAM.gov registration status, and federal registrations
  • Add key personnel, board members, and organizational capacity details
The more you tell us, the better your AI-generated content and the more accurate your eligibility matching.
2

Find Your Grant Free

Use our research tools to find the grants you actually qualify for. No more wasting weeks chasing grants you were never eligible for.

  • Use the Grant Finder Wizard — answer 3 questions and get ranked matches based on your organization profile
  • Or browse the full database with filters: agency, category, funding amount, deadline
  • Or search by keyword across thousands of federal grant opportunities
  • Eligibility screening automatically checks which grants your organization qualifies for
  • Ineligible grants are clearly flagged so you can focus your time on real opportunities
Ineligible grants are flagged so you don't waste time on grants you can't get.
3

Build Your Budget $19.95/mo

The Budget Builder creates a detailed, accurate budget that becomes the single source of truth for your entire application. Every section references these exact numbers.

  • Use the Budget Builder to enter your actual line items by category
  • Personnel: name each person, their role, level of effort (%), and salary
  • The system auto-calculates fringe benefits, MTDC (Modified Total Direct Costs), and indirect costs based on your rate
  • Add travel, equipment, supplies, contractual, and other direct cost categories
  • Your budget becomes the single source of truth — every narrative section references these exact numbers
No more budget inconsistencies across sections. Change a number once, and it updates everywhere.
4

Generate Your Application $19.95/mo

AI writes each section using your organization data, your budget numbers, and the specific agency template. You review and refine — the AI drafts, you make it yours.

  • AI generates each section using your org profile + budget data + the agency's template requirements
  • Each section sees the previous ones — consistent project title, personnel names, and budget numbers throughout
  • Review and edit each section directly in the editor
  • Character and word counter shows you're within page limits before you submit
  • Regenerate any section if you want a different approach or emphasis
The AI drafts, you refine. Every section is specific to YOUR organization, YOUR budget, and YOUR project.
5

Complete the Checklist $19.95/mo

The submission checklist tracks every single requirement — forms, narrative sections, supporting documents, and certifications. Nothing gets missed.

  • Submission checklist tracks EVERY requirement: SF-424 forms, narrative sections, supporting documents, and certifications
  • Upload letters of support, MOUs, organizational charts, resumes, and other attachments
  • AI can draft MOUs and letters of support for you to customize and send to partners
  • Self-certify required items: SAM.gov active registration, Grants.gov account created, board authorization obtained
  • Visual progress bar shows exactly how close you are to a complete submission package
Nothing submits until every box is checked — no missing forms, no incomplete sections, no last-minute scrambles.
6

Final Consistency Check $19.95/mo

Before you submit, our AI reviewer reads your entire application and checks for the cross-section inconsistencies that get grants rejected.

  • AI reviews the entire application for cross-section consistency
  • Checks budget math: do personnel costs match the budget justification? Do totals add up?
  • Checks consistency: project title, personnel names, indirect cost rate, and timeline match across all sections
  • Rule-based validation catches page limits, missing required sections, and funding amount mismatches
  • Get a clear report of issues to fix before submission
Our AI reviewer catches the mistakes that get grants rejected — before you submit.
7

Generate & Submit $19.95/mo

One click generates your complete submission package. Download it, print it, or use our guided submission mode to paste directly into the grant portal.

  • Download a final PDF (unbranded, clean formatting) for paper or email submission
  • Use Guided Submission for online portals — copy-paste each field with progress tracking
  • Download as DOCX for editing in Word or Google Docs
  • Download as TXT for plain text portal fields
  • Print a draft anytime for board review or internal sign-off
One click generates your complete submission package — formatted, consistent, and ready to go.

8

Track & Reuse $19.95/mo

After submission, track your application status and reuse your work for the next funding cycle. The hardest grant is the first one — after that, you clone and update.

  • Mark as submitted with your confirmation number for your records
  • Track status through the lifecycle: submitted, under review, funded, or rejected
  • Clone any application for next year's funding cycle — update the budget and timeline, keep the narrative
  • Deadline reminders with calendar export so you never miss a resubmission window
The hardest grant is the first one. After that, you clone, update, and resubmit in a fraction of the time.
