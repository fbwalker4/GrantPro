Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free tier?

Search the full grant database, use the Grant Finder Wizard, check eligibility, and save favorites — all completely free. Upgrade to a paid plan when you're ready to write and submit.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. No cancellation fees or long-term commitments.

Do you guarantee I'll get funded?

No grant service can guarantee funding — that's determined by the agency. What we do is give you the best tools and guidance to submit the strongest application possible.

What agencies are supported?

All 21 agencies: NSF, DOE, NIH, USDA, HUD, EPA, DOT, NIST, ED, HHS, SAMHSA, DOJ, DOL, NASA, DOD, FEMA, NEA, SBA, DOC, DOI, and VA. We add new templates based on user requests.

What counts as a "submission"?

A submission is a completed grant application package generated for download or guided submission. Drafts, edits, and searches do not count toward your monthly limit.
