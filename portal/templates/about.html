About Grant Pro

Grant Pro helps organizations discover and win more funding through AI-powered grant research and writing tools.

Our Mission

We believe that securing grant funding shouldn't require weeks of research or expensive consultants. Grant Pro puts powerful grant discovery and writing tools in the hands of organizations that need them most — small businesses, nonprofits, and public agencies working to make a difference in their communities.

How We Help

🔍

Grant Research

Search hundreds of federal, state, and private grants. Filter by eligibility, amount, deadline, and focus area.

📝

AI Writing Assistance

Get AI-generated drafts for each section of your grant application, tailored to specific agency requirements.

📄

Agency Templates

Pre-built templates for NSF, DOE, NIH, USDA, HUD, EPA, and more — with agency-specific guidance built in.
